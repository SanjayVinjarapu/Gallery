About us

Lord VISHWAKARMA is considered by followers of the Hindu faith to be the divine architect or engineer of the universe. Drawing inspiration from Lord Viswakarma, his descendants are renowned for their outstanding architectural skills. The ancient temples of Sri Kalahasthi, Tirupathi, Srisailam, Lepakshi, Amaravathi, 1000 pillars, Alampur, Arasavilli, Bhadrachalam and Ramappa in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh bear ample testimony to the precision, skills and aesthetic craftsmanship of the Vishwabrahmins.

In modern times, the Vishwabrahmins have diversified their occupations from the building trade to embrace newer professions like medicine, engineering, law, management, governance, arts and education, among others, in tune with the trends of the present day. However, although the Vishwabrahmins have been making a substantial contribution to the social, cultural and economic fields through the ages, recognition for their contribution to the well-being of the Telugu speaking people is inadequately acknowledged.

There is therefore, a collective effort that is needed to rejuvenate the Viswakarma community with planned and programmed interventions, a responsibility that the Vishwakarma Foundation and Seva Trust, founded by eminent members of the Vishwabrahmin community, has been shouldering since in 2014. Since then, every year new activities are added to serve community members optimally and improve, after a detailed consultative process.

Activities

  • To achieve a distinct identity for the temple of the Viswakarma community in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh as well as globally by establishing Community Centres to create avenues of social interaction and sustain the rich and varied cultural heritage of the community.

  • To uplift the educational standards of the Vishwakarma community and assist Vishwabrahmins reach the commanding heights of modern professions and gain recognition for their contributions through establishment institutes of educational excellence and hostels with all amenities to provide a home away from home atmosphere.

  • To provide health security for all members of the community by establishing affordable healthcare facilities, especially the vulnerable sections like the aged, women and children.

  • To provide shelter to the needy members of the community – the aged, the orphans and the poor – so that they enjoy a better quality of life in their hour of need.

  • To provide financial and material assistance to the neediest of needy members of the community – students, widows, orphans, the abandoned elderly – to sustain livelihoods and career goals.

  • To provide ideal match-making by offering a variety of matrimonial services from matching horoscopes to wedding planning and services.

  • To empower women members of the community to explore avenues of higher education, self-employment and employment.

  • To share the divine knowledge of Vishwakarma Tathwam, Vishwakarma Sahityam and the History and Culture of the Vishwakarmas through special training programmes.

  • To empower and assist through a charitable welfare outreach program for the benefit of economically and socially backward communities – SC, ST, BC and minorities – as a socially responsible and responsible organization.
