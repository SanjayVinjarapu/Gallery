President Message

Dr. Golthi Mallikarjun Rao is the founder and President of the Vishwakarma Foundation and Seva Trust.

Dear Brahmasri Vishwakarman including friends and well-wishers

Namaskaram..!

My name is Dr. Golthi Mallikarjun Rao and I am the Founder - President of VKFST. It is with great pleasure that we welcome each and every one of you visitors on behalf of Vishwakarma Foundation and Seva Trust (VKFST) to our website.
Having acquired around 37 years of experience in the field higher education, I along with many like minded community members have come together to serve this organization.

VKFST is a charitable organization that stands for Vishwakarmaâ€™s operating from Hyderabad in the state of Telangana. The members of this organization have joined together from the state of Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, other States of India and also members across the globe. This organization is initiated to serve the backward, under privileged classes of the society in particular the Vishwabrahmin community, in the areas of education, culture, empowerment, health, alliance services, shelters for senior citizens, students and award of financial assistance to the needy and deserving students of Vishwabrahmin community and other backward communities. VKFST is determined to conduct activities in the above said areas. Hence, this foundation has been established on 18th February 2014 vide registration # 33/IV/2014 and amended the document in 22.12.2015 vide registration # 251/IV/2015.


We are providing scholarships regularly to the needy students, who are pursuing higher education. So far we have provided scholarship to the following:

1. Ms. Jyotsna for MBA
2. Mr. Anupoju Revanth for BBA
3. Ms. Ramoju Haripriya for B.Com.
4. Ms. Uppala Bhavani for B.Com.
5. Ms. Taruni Sandhya for B.Com.

Since then, every year new activities are added to serve community members optimally and improve, after a detailed consultative process. Today, the activities of the Vishwakarma Foundation and Seva Trust are focused at various levels. We would like to thank all those of you who supports for VKFST. We really appreciate each and every effort. We also welcome all those of your who have joined us recently.We welcome all to join in this VKFST to serve for the greater success of our community. We request each and every one of you to personally reach out to me and let me know your contributions, feedback and opinions regarding the direction, mission and vision of the organization. In the days to come our foundation is determined to establish educational institutions, Shelters for Senior Citizens, Students, and Scholarships to students regularly.
