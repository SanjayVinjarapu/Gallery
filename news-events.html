News & Events

In recently concluded function at Ravindra Bharati main hall on 19th March, several dignitaries were present and spoke about the importance of Education and services of VKFST to promote education by supporting poor students of vishwabrahmana community.

  • Padmasri Velu Anand Acharya, Eminent Stapathi, Telangana State
  • Sri KM Kiran Kumar Garu ACP, EOW Central Crime Station, Basheerbagh
  • Sri Varaprasad Gurajapu, Senior Director L&T, USA
  • Sri Vinod Kumar Kukunoor, KAVIN Friends Foundation, USA
  • Dr Vangala Shantikrishna, Guinness Book of World record holder in Telangana Folk Arts
  • Dr Tumpala Venkateshwara Rao, Industrialist
  • Sri PVRK Prasad, General Manager, HMMWSB
  • Sri Dakoju Balaji, Vruksha Bandhu, Environmentalist
  • Sri PVRK Prasad and Dr TV Rao garu were awarded Golden Donor awards for their generous donations to VKFST Education fund called VIDYA SEVA FUND
  • Following Students were awarded scholarships for their fees payment in this function:
    1. Kalishetti Geeta Charan
    2. Patnala Bhavya
    3. Kanuru Venkata Nagendra
    4. Billakurti Leelakrishna
